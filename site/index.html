Your files. Your photos. Your machine. Nothing leaves the laptop.

How it works

1

Index

Gemma 4 describes and categorizes your photos, documents, videos, and notes.

2

Embed

Descriptions are converted to vectors and stored locally in SQLite.

3

Search

Queries are matched instantly via semantic similarity. No LLM call needed.

Features

  • Photos, docs, videos, notes
  • Apple Photos integration
  • Auto-categorization
  • Background file watching
  • Choose your embedding model
  • 100% local — powered by Ollama

Get started

$ pip install cidre

$ cidre init

$ cidre add ~/Documents

$ cidre add --photos

$ cidre index

$ cidre search "sunset photos"
